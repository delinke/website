Bang Bang!

Illustration - Graphic Design - Pattern Design


In this experiment I've been inspired by a childhood memory. My grandmother had tapestries with Hungarian motifs on her wall. I really didn't like them as a kid, as they didn't make any sense to me. Now it's so delightful to recall that memory. The ludicrously simple way the persimmon orange string decorated the plain white fabric. I have reimagined that tapestry, keeping some of the motifs and colours, and adding some new ones, to create a funky wall decoration.
