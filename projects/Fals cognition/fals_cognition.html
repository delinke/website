Fals cognition

Calligraphy - Graphic Design - Typography


It's a calligraphy, typography experiment inspired by my eternal issue of mixing the words gyors and gyros. During my daily walk to work in Hungary I used to pass a currency exchange point with a flashing neon sign that read "gyors", a Hungarian word meaning: 1.rash 2.rapid 3.quick 4.speedy. However, anytime my eye passed over the sign I was reading gyros, a Greek dish much like a kebab. Meanwhile, for the gyros takeaway restaurant, I started reading gyors forever more. This really affected me so much, and it was so annoying that I needed to react. Here is my reaction with other words, as it seems that this phenomenon happens with me quite often.
